# Dataset Contributors — Consent Log

Every signer whose landmark data enters `data/` is recorded here
(DATA_GUIDELINES.md §2). Real names are NOT stored in this repo — the
signer-ID ↔ name mapping stays offline with the maintainer.

By consenting, a contributor confirms they understand:

1. Only **hand/body landmark coordinates** (numbers) are recorded — never
   images or video; no face or identity can be reconstructed from the data.
2. The data becomes part of a **public open-source dataset** in this repository.
3. They may **withdraw at any time**: their signer ID's rows are deleted and
   models are retrained at the next release.
